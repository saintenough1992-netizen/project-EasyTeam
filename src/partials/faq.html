Frequently Asked Questions

Here I've gathered answers to the most popular questions to help you plan your perfect day. If you can't find the information you're looking for, please get in touch!

  • How do we book our date and how far in advance should we do it?

    It's best to book your date 6-12 months in advance, especially if your wedding is during the popular season. To book your date, simply write to me through the contact form. We'll arrange a meeting, discuss all the details, sign a contract, and after the retainer is paid, your date will be officially reserved for you.

  • We don't know how to pose and feel awkward in front of the camera. Do you help with that?

    Of course, and that's completely normal! My main goal is to create a comfortable and relaxed atmosphere for you. I don't force you into unnatural poses. Instead, I provide gentle guidance and direction to capture your real emotions, smiles, and tender glances. The most important thing is for you to be yourselves, and I'll take care of the rest.

  • When will we receive our photos and how many will there be?

    I understand how excited you are to see the results! You will receive a small preview of the best photos (10-15 images) within the first week after your wedding. The complete gallery of photos will be delivered via a personal online gallery within 4-8 weeks. For a full wedding day, you will receive 500+ carefully edited images.

  • What is typically included in your wedding packages?

    Each package includes my services for the agreed-upon time, a detailed pre-wedding consultation, meticulous editing of all photos, and access to a private online gallery where you can download your images in high resolution. For detailed information on all packages and pricing, please get in touch with me.

  • Do you travel for weddings outside of Berlin?

    Yes, absolutely! I love to travel and discover new beautiful locations for photoshoots. I would be happy to travel to any city in Ukraine or even abroad to be a part of your celebration. Travel terms and costs are discussed individually.
